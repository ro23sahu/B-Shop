About Us

Welcome to B-Clothing , your number one source for Clothing. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of Cloths, with a focus on three characteristics, ie: Good Products, customer Satisfaction and Best Deals For you.
Founded in 2021 by Mr.Rama, B-clothin has come a long way from its beginnings. When Mr.Rama first started out, his passion for helping other parents be more eco-friendly, providing the best equipment for his fellow musicians drove him to do intense research, quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store.
We now serve customers all over India, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the fashion industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Mr.Rama, B-clothing,Founder.
